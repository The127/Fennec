## Codebase Patterns

- Domain classes in `Fennec.App/Domain/` are pure static classes with no Avalonia/CommunityToolkit.Mvvm dependencies.
- Domain tests in `Fennec.App.Tests/Domain/` use xUnit.
- NodaTime `Instant` comparisons in tests must use noon UTC (e.g. `Instant.FromUtc(2024, 1, day, 12, 0)`) to avoid timezone-sensitive day-boundary failures on systems ahead of UTC.
